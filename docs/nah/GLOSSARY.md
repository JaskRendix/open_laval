# **Nahuatl Aerodynamics Glossary**

A Classical Nahuatl glossary for aerodynamic and turbomachinery terminology used in the OpenLaval documentation.

---

## **Fundamental Flow Concepts**

- **ēhecatl‑yōlōtl** — airflow  
  *“the heart/life of the wind”*

- **āyōtl‑tlahpani** — supersonic  
  *“that which breaks through sound”*

- **Māch‑tlamantli** — Mach number  
  *borrowed root + classifier (“Mach quantity”)*

- **ēhecatl‑tlalpan** — flow field  
  *“wind‑domain”*

---

## **Shock, Expansion, and PM Angles**

- **tlatlaminaliztli** — shock wave  
  *“striking/impacting motion”*

- **tlatlapaliztli** — expansion wave  
  *“opening/spreading motion”*

- **Prandtl–Meyer tlapalehuiliztli** — Prandtl–Meyer angle  
  *“turning/deflection measure”*

- **tlapalehuiliztli ome‑ixiptla** — asymmetric PM angles  
  *“two‑form angle”*

---

## **Turbine Blade Geometry**

- **tēpoztli‑īxiptla** — blade profile  
  *“metal form/surface”*

- **īpan tlacuilolli** — upper surface  
  *“upper drawing/line”*

- **īcxitlan tlacuilolli** — lower surface  
  *“underside/foot‑side drawing”*

- **ome‑ixiptla tēpoztli** — asymmetric blade  
  *“two‑aspect metal form”*

- **tlatlapaliztli‑quixpolōlli** — shock‑cancelling curve  
  *“expansion‑cancellation curve”*

- **tlatzintla‑tlacuilolli** — arc segment  
  *“curved‑line drawing”*

- **tlayecantli‑tlacuilolli** — straight‑line region  
  *“front‑facing line”*

- **tlayacantli‑tlatequi** — leading‑edge trimming  
  *“front‑edge cutting”*

- **huehcatl tēpoztli** — blade height  
  *“height of the metal”*

---

## **Characteristic Curves and R\***

- **R‑cuahcualtzin** — R\*  
  *“curved‑measure property”*

- **R‑cuahcualtzin ome‑ixiptla** — asymmetric R\*  
  *“two‑form curved‑measure”*

---

## **Gas Properties**

- **āyōtl‑tlapōhualli** — gas  
  *“sound‑substance” (scientific calque)*

- **tlālli‑tōnaliztli** — specific heat ratio γ  
  *“heat‑nature measure”*

- **tlatzōntli** — pressure  
  *“force/weight”*

- **tlatzintli** — density  
  *“compactness”*

- **yōlōtl‑mōtlahtōl** — velocity  
  *“movement of life/energy”*

---

## **Angles and Turning**

- **tlapalehuiliztli** — angle  
  *“tilt/turning”*

- **tlayecantli tlapalehuiliztli** — inlet angle  
  *“front‑side turning”*

- **tlatzintlan tlapalehuiliztli** — outlet angle  
  *“back‑side turning”*
